1. The right arrow is used to move the space ship on right direction.
  2. The left arrow is used to move the plane on left direction.
  3. The up arrow is used to move the plane on up direction.
  4. The down arrow is used to move the plane on down direction.
  5. The letter d used to move the space ship on right direction.
  6. The letter a is used to move the plane on left direction.
  7. The letter w is used to move the plane on up direction.
  8. The letter s is used to move the plane on down direction.
  9. Use the space to shoot the rocks.

Instructions of the game :

You should try not to make the rocks bump your space ship or you lose one of your heart if you lose your lives . it is game over. if you get stars it increase your heart. the level of this game is higher by default, when you are in the higher level the rocks will be down faster. if you shoot the star or you can't catch it your lives not increase.
